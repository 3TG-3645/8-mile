What is 8 Mile?

8 mile is a movie that stars Eminem as the main character, playing as a character created for the movie known as B-Rabbit. B-Rabbit is a character entirely inspired on the rapper Eminem and played by himself. 8 Mile's name comes from the city of Detroit from the same name. where Marshall Mathers came out from.

Lose Yourself and where did it originate from?

Lose Yourself is a song made for the movie 8 Mile. it plays on the end credits of the movie and it's the most famous song of the rapper. the song talks about the struggles of the main character and the will for success. matter of fact, after this, Eminem won an oscar for that movie being the first rapper to get an oscar ever.
